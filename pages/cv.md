But current state of the art in control assumes that disturbances from the environment are minimal. However, the effects of wind, waves, and rough terrain may make it difficult for the vehicle to   a steady base for the manipulator. Or, in some cases, the vehicle may lack the control authority to negate disturbances in all directions.
